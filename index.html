Bri Blair

My personal journal website to describe my thoughts and give my perspective on current events as well as publish my essays, reflections, notes, and honest revisions on who it is that I'm becoming! This site was inspired by my personal journal(s)!

I've been keeping journals for roughly 7 years now, and have accumulated over 17 different physical journal notebooks, all filled with different thoughts from different eras of my life. Sometimes I feel like my best writing is done in my journal, and I'd like to share those pieces with others.

My hope for this website is that people, even if a small audience, can feel seen and/or relate to some of my thoughts. I also plan to keep this website as a digital record of my personal progress.
